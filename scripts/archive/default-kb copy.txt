## 9. Dojo Etiquette & Rules

### General Rules
- Bow when entering/exiting the mat.  
- Keep uniforms clean and tied properly.  
- No shoes on the training floor.  
- No gum, food, or phones on the mats.  
- Fingernails/toenails must be trimmed.  
- Respect instructors and fellow students.  

### Parent Guidelines
- Parents may observe from the viewing area.  
- Please avoid distracting students during class.
